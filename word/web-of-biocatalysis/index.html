Web of Biocatalysis

A living backbone of the field, continuously updated and re-interpreted.

What is this?

The Web of Biocatalysis is not a frozen review article.
It is a living map: connecting pillars of knowledge, highlighting key papers,
tracking trends, and linking to curated data.

  • It grows over time.
  • It updates with new insights.
  • It shows both history and horizon.
